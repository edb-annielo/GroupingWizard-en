Grouping Wizard - a lucky draw and grouping tool

Version:   (Download App or Update)

This online application, developed by ITE Section of EDB, allows to create instant groupings and lucky draw activities with easy steps. This tool can help to split students into groups according to the set criteria for various purposes such as group discussion and project activities. Grouping criteria can be set to suit the needs of different activities. The grouping and draw results can be downloaded.
